Finding Peace Amidst of Chaos

Feeling weight down by everyday stress and demanding schedules?

This 12 second meditation which can be repeated as many times as needed, will bring you to the present moment. By breathing slowly, deeply and calmly racing thoughts and feelings will slow down. This meditation will immediately reduce the feelings of anxiety, stress, improve focus and provide a mental reset.

Step 1:

Ease into the Rythm

Focus on the glowy orange orb. Don't try to fall into a breathing pattern yet. Just bring your attention to as it increase and decreases in size, naturally easing into the rhythm.

Step 2:

Inhale & Exhale

Once you have noticed the rhythm. Inhale for 6 seconds as the orb expands. Hold your breathe for 1 second. Then exhale your breathe for 6 seconds and the orb contracts.

Step 3:

Repeat

Repeat this mediation for as long as you need to. By bringing your focus to your breathing and glowing orb. All other thoughts and feelings will fade into the background.
